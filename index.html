Welcome to Constella, an open-universe space exploration and combat game. Find your fortune in the stars, fend off the various threats and horrors within the cosmos, and discover the secrets and stories of this galaxy. The game is currently in early access. Apply to participate in the community server!

The game is currently in an early access program, a limited release with open signups until slots are filled. The sign-up sheet is still open, and will remain open until slots are filled! If you want to give the game a go, join the development group and fill out the sign-up form.
